As we dig into abstract vector spaces, I expect you are able to prove
various results by invoking the axioms.

\begin{problem}
  In a vector space, the additive identity is unique.
\end{problem}

\vfill

\begin{problem}
  $0 \cdot v = \vec{0}$.
\end{problem}

\vfill

\begin{problem}
  $\lambda \cdot \vec{0} = \vec{0}$.
\end{problem}

\vfill

\begin{problem}
  $-1 \cdot v = -v$.
\end{problem}
